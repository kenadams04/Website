This Is The Timeline Representation Of Various Achievements By SOI So Far.......

July 2018

A colony being established on Mars.

Feb 2018

The Rocket with 7 humans which was sent towards Mars has successfully reached the Red Planet.
This is among the biggest achievments by SOI.

May 2017

On 21st May 2017, SOI's Rover Discovered Water Content on Mars. This was one of the greatest achievements in the history.

Sept 2016

SOI sending humans on Red Planet for the first time.
A rocket with 7 humans launched recently with the aim to establish a human colony on Mars.

June 2016

The Rover reached the Red Planet Successfully.
With this achievement, India joins a club of deep space explorers such as the United States, Russia and Europe, all of which have reached Mars. The success is also a reason for national pride as India became the first single nation to reach the Red Planet and enter orbit at its first go.
